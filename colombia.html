A Tour of My Hometown

Another project I had worked on during my Graphic Desgin in Cartography course (GEOG 572) was to create a roadmap of all the landmarks in your hometown. This project required me to venture out around my hometown to find out what makes my hometown special, and so I took on that challenge!

My hometown of Blaine, Minnesota is a typical urban sprawling suburb. Although it does not have much in regards to historical landmarks, it more than makes up for it in its own cultural way: sports! Sport culture is big in the United States, and since my city has a plethora of sporting venues (with plent more to come in the near future), I thought it would be a great topic for this challenge!

For this challenge, I used ArcGIS Online to create a storymap highlighting each chosen location. I had also used Adobe Photoshop to create a little collage of all the pictures I gathered out in the field (aside a select few locations). Together, this storymap highlights what makes my hometown special!
