The Story of Environmental Scandal: The Forever Chemical Conundrum

My first challenge while taking my first master's course, which was Graphic Desgin in Cartography (GEOG 572), was to create a story using an ArcGIS Online storymap. I accepted the challenge and dove deeper into a story I had heard about years ago...

This story I had read talked about how the company 3M had to pay hundreds of millions of dollars to both state and local governments for their contamination of groundwater on the east side of the Minneapolis metro. This story stuck with me ever since, which in turn made me ask many questions.

One question that sparked my interest was: has this effected the people's health in any way? I had done investigation work and used ArcGIS Pro to help answer these questions through attribute editing and through creating different maps, such as a chloropleth map.
